Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

DLP Policy Tester ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a top priority. This policy explains how we handle your information - or rather, how we don't handle it, because we don't collect any.

Simple Summary: Your data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit anything. Period.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Text you enter for testing
  • Scan results
  • Usage patterns or analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Contact information

Data Processing

All pattern matching and data detection occurs entirely on your device using local processing. The App operates completely offline - no internet connection is required or used for scanning operations.

Data Storage

The App does not permanently store any data. Text entered into the app exists only in memory during your session. When you close the app or clear the input, all data is immediately removed from memory.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No cloud services
  • No social media integrations

Network Activity

The App does not make any network requests. It does not connect to the internet for any purpose.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for professional use by IT administrators, security professionals, and compliance teams. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over any data you enter into the App, and it never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy with a new "Last Updated" date. Any changes will be posted on this page. Since we don't collect data, we have no way to notify you directly of changes.

Security

Since all data processing happens locally on your device and we don't transmit or store any information, your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using device passcode/biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not using real sensitive data for testing purposes

Support & Help

Getting Started

DLP Policy Tester helps you test Data Loss Prevention policies by scanning text for sensitive information patterns.

How to Use the App

  1. Enter Text: Type or paste text containing potential sensitive data into the input field
  2. Select Detection Type: Choose "Scan All Types" or select a specific data type from the dropdown
  3. Tap Scan: Press the "Scan for Sensitive Data" button
  4. Review Results: View detected patterns with risk assessment and detailed matches
  5. Expand Details: Tap on any result card to see individual matches and pattern descriptions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why wasn't my pattern detected?

The app includes smart validation logic to reduce false positives:

  • SSNs: Numbers starting with 000, 666, or 900-999 are rejected as invalid
  • Credit Cards: Must pass Luhn algorithm validation
  • Phone Numbers: Must match valid US formats

Can I test custom patterns?

Yes! Select "Custom Pattern" from the Detection Type dropdown, enter your regex pattern, and tap Scan.

What data types are supported?

The app can detect:

  • Social Security Numbers (SSN)
  • Credit Card Numbers (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover)
  • Email Addresses
  • Phone Numbers (US format)
  • IP Addresses (IPv4)
  • Driver's License Numbers
  • Passport Numbers (US format)
  • Bank Account Numbers
  • Custom Regex Patterns

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally on your device. No data is transmitted over the network or stored permanently.

Can I use real sensitive data?

No! This app is for testing and educational purposes only. Always use fictional or sample data. The app clearly states this is not for use with real sensitive information.

What do the risk levels mean?

  • No Risk: No sensitive data detected
  • Low Risk: 1-2 matches found
  • Medium Risk: 3-5 matches found
  • High Risk: 6+ matches found

How do I clear my data?

Tap the "Clear" button to remove all text from the input field. You can also close and reopen the app - nothing persists between sessions.

The app isn't working correctly

Try these steps:

  1. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  2. Close and restart the app
  3. Check that your test data matches expected formats
  4. If scanning large amounts of text, try smaller chunks
